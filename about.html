About Hangman

What is Hangman?

  Hangman is a guessing game for two or more players. One player thinks of a word, phrase, or sentence and the other(s) tries to guess it by suggesting letters or numbers within a certain number of guesses.

  To learn more about Hangman, you can visit Wikipedia.

How to Play?

  1. A word is chosen randomly, and its letters are hidden.
  2. Guess a letter by clicking on the corresponding button (At first the best choice is vowels).
  3. If the letter is part of the word, it will be revealed in its correct position(s).
  4. If the letter is not part of the word, a part of a hangman's body will be drawn.
  5. Keep guessing letters until you either guess the word or the hangman is complete.
  6. You win if you guess the word before the hangman is complete.
  7. You lose if the hangman is complete before you guess the word.
  8. Have fun playing Hangman!

What are Hangman benefits of playing?

  • Vocabulary expansion and spelling improvement
  • The game engages your brain, requiring you to think critically, make educated guesses, and strategize as you try to uncover the word.
  • Patience and Perseverance:
  • It is Educational Tool and make learning more interactive and engaging.
  • Entertainment and Relaxation
